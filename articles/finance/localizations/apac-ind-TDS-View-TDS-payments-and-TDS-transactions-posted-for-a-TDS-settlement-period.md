[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="a13d6-104">Šioje temoje paaiškinama, kaip peržiūrėti iš šaltinio (TDS) atskaičius mokesčius ir užregistruotas sudengimo laikotarpio operacijas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-104">This topic explains how to view the Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) payments and transactions that were posted for a settlement period.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-105">Pasirinkite **Mokestis \> Netiesioginiai mokesčiai \> Išskaitomas mokestis \> Išskaitomo mokesčio sudengimo laikotarpiai**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-105">Go to **Tax \> Indirect taxes \> Withholding tax \> Withholding tax settlement periods**.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-106">[![Išskaitomo mokesčio sudengimo laikotarpių puslapis](./media/apac-ind-TDS-50.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-50.png)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-106">[![Withholding tax settlement periods page](./media/apac-ind-TDS-50.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-50.png)</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-107">Puslapyje **Išskaitomo mokesčio sudengimo laikotarpiai** rinkitės **Išskaitomo mokesčio mokėjimai** norėdami atidaryti **Išskaitomo mokesčio mokėjimų** puslapį, kuriame galite peržiūrėti TDS sudengimą, kurie buvo atlikti tam tikrą TDS sudengimo laikotarpį.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-107">On the **Withholding tax settlement periods** page, select **Withholding tax payments** to open the **Withholding tax payments** page, where you can view the TDS settlements that were made for a specific TDS settlement period.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-108">Skirtuke **Peržiūra** įveskite toliau nurodytą informaciją:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-108">The **Overview** tab shows the following information:</span></span>

    - <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-109">**Data** – TDS sudengimo data.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-109">**Date** – The date of TDS settlement.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-110">**Kvitas** – TDS sudengimo operacijos kvito numeris</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-110">**Voucher** – The voucher number of the TDS settlement transaction.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-111">**Mokesčio tipas** – mokesčio tipas, pagal kurį vykdomas sudengimo procesas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-111">**Tax type** – The tax type that the settlement process is run for.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-112">**Mokesčių sąskaitos numeris (TAN)** – sudengtos TDS operacijos mokesčių sąskaitos numeris (TAN).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-112">**Tax Account Number (TAN)** – The Tax Account Number (TAN) of the settled TDS transaction.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-113">Lauke **Sudengimo laikotarpis** sudengimo laikotarpis, per kurį vyksta TDS sudengimo procesas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-113">**Settlement period** – The settlement period that the TDS settlement process is run for.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-114">**Pradžios data** – sudengimo laikotarpio pradžia.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-114">**From date** – The start date of the settlement period.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-115">**Pabaigos data** – sudengimo laikotarpio pabaiga.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-115">**To date** – The end date of the settlement period.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-116">**Išskaitomo mokesčio mokėjimo versija** – išskaitomo mokesčio mokėjimo versija: **Originalas**, **Paskutiniai pataisymai** arba **Bendrasis sąrašas**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-116">**Withholding tax payment version** – The version of the withholding tax payment: **Original**, **Latest corrections**, or **Total list**.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-117">Pasirinkite **kvitą**, norėdami peržiūrėti TDS operacijos kvitų įrašus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-117">Select **Voucher** to view the voucher entries for the TDS transaction.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-118">Norėdami **peržiūrėti išsamią informaciją** apie TDS operacijas, sudengtas konkrečiu laikotarpiu sudengimo laikotarpiu, pasirinkite Išskaitomo mokesčio operacijas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-118">Select **Withholding tax transactions** to view the details of the TDS transactions that were settled for a specific period during a settlement period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-119">Pasirinkite **kvitą**, norėdami peržiūrėti TDS operacijos kvitų įrašus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-119">Select **Voucher** to view the voucher entries for the TDS transaction.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-120">Norėdami peržiūrėti TDS, apskaičiuotą pagal konkretaus TDS mokesčio kodo TDS mokesčio komponentą, pasirinkite **išskaitomo mokesčio komponentus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-120">Select **Withholding tax components** to view the TDS that was calculated per TDS tax component for a specific TDS tax code.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-121">Norėdami grįžti **į išskaitomo mokesčio sudengimo** laikotarpių puslapį **uždarykite išskaitomo mokesčio mokėjimų** puslapį.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-121">Close the **Withholding tax payments** page to return to the **Withholding tax settlement periods** page.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="a13d6-122">Norėdami **peržiūrėti išsamią informaciją** apie TDS operacijas, sudengtas konkrečiu laikotarpiu sudengimo laikotarpiu, pasirinkite Išskaitomo mokesčio operacijas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a13d6-122">Select **Withholding tax transactions** to view the details of the TDS transactions that were settled for the settlement period.</span></span>
