# <a name="record-tds-recoverable-certificate-numbers"></a><span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-103">Įrašykite TDS keičiamas sertifikato numerius</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-103">Record TDS recoverable certificate numbers</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-104">Šioje temoje paaiškinama, kaip naudoti **Susigrąžinamų sertifikatų** puslapį norint įrašyti sertifikatų numerius ir datas, taikomas šaltinio (TDS) sertifikatams, kurie gauti konkrečiam tiekėjui, klientui arba DK.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-104">This topic explains how to use the **Recoverable certificates** page to record the certificate numbers and dates for Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) certificates that are received for a specific vendor, customer, or ledger.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-105">Norėdami atnaujinti TDS sertifikatų numerius ir datas, įrašytus šio puslapio TDS operacijoms, naudokite puslapį **Atnaujinti sertifikatą** (**DK \> Periodinis \> išskaitomo mokesčio \> atnaujinimo sertifikatas**).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-105">To update the TDS certificate numbers and dates that are recorded for TDS transactions on this page, use the **Update certificate** page (**General ledger \> Periodic \> Withholding tax \> Update certificate**).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-106">Baigę atnaujinti TDS sertifikatų numerius, uždarykite juos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-106">After you've finished updating TDS certificate numbers, close them.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-107">Norėdami įrašyti TDS sertifikatų numerius ir datas, atlikite šiuos veiksmus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-107">Follow these steps to record the TDS certificate numbers and dates.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-108">Pasirinkite **Mokestis \> Netiesioginiai mokesčiai \> Išskaitomas mokestis \> Keičiamas sertifikatas**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-108">Go to **Tax \> Indirect tax \> Withholding tax \> Recoverable certificates**.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-109">[![Atkuriamų sertifikatų puslapis](./media/apac-ind-TDS-49.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-49.png)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-109">[![Recoverable certificates page](./media/apac-ind-TDS-49.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-49.png)</span></span> 

2. <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-110">Puslapyje **Atkurti sertifikatai** plaukelyje **Mokesčio tipas** rinkitės **TDS**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-110">On the **Recoverable certificates** page, in the **Tax type** field, select **TDS**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-111">Pasirinkite **Naujas** pranešimui sukurti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-111">Select **New** to create a record.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-112">Lauke **Sertifikato numeris** įveskite TDS koncesijos sertifikato numerį.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-112">In the **Certificate number** field, enter the TDS certificate number.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-113">Lauke **Sąskaitos tipas** pasirinkite sąskaitos, kurią TDS sertifikatas gavo, tipą: **Tiekėjas**, **klientas** arba **DK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-113">In the **Account type** field, select the type of account that the TDS certificate is received for: **Vendor**, **Customer**, or **Ledger**.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-114">Lauke **Sąskaita** pasirinkite tiekėjo, kliento arba DK sąskaitos numerį, atsižvelgdami į pasirinktą sąskaitos tipą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-114">In the **Account** field, select the vendor, customer, or ledger account number, depending on the account type that you selected.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-115">Lauke **Pavadinimas** rodomas tiekėjo, kliento arba DK sąskaitos pavadinimas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-115">The **Name** field shows the name of the vendor, customer, or ledger account.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-116">Laukelyje **Sertifikato suma** įveskite TDS sertifikato sumą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-116">In the **Certificate amount** field, enter the amount of the TDS certificate.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-117">Lauke **Data** įveskite sertifikato data jo numeriui.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-117">In the **Date** field, enter the certificate date for the certificate number.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-118">Rinkitės **Užklausos** norėdami atidaryti **sertifikato operacijų** puslapį, kuriame galite peržiūrėti TDS operacijas, kurių TDS sertifikato numeris ir data yra atnaujinami.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-118">Select **Inquiries** to open the **Certificate transactions** page, where you can view the TDS transactions that the TDS certificate number and date are updated for.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-119">Šią informaciją galima atnaujinti puslapyje **Naujinti sertifikatą** (**Mokesčių \> deklaracijų \> išskaitomo mokesčio \> Naujinti sertifikatą**).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-119">This information can be updated on the **Update certificate** page (**Tax \> Declarations \> Withholding tax \> Update certificate**).</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-120">Atnaujinimo **sertifikato puslapyje** rodoma ši kiekvienos TDS operacijos informacija:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-120">The **Update certificate** page shows the following information for each TDS transaction:</span></span>

    - <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-121">**Data** – TDS operacijos registravimo data.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-121">**Date** – The posting date of the TDS transaction.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-122">**Kvitas** – TDS operacijos kvito numeris</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-122">**Voucher** – The voucher number of the TDS transaction.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-123">**Šaltinis** – modulis, kuriame sukurta TDS operacija.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-123">**Source** – The module that the TDS transaction was created in.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-124">**Sąskaita** – tiekėjo, kliento ar DK sąskaitos numeris, kuriam buvo sukurta TDS operacija.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-124">**Account** – The vendor, customer, or ledger account number that the TDS transaction was created for.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-125">**Pavadinimas** – tiekėjo, kliento ar DK sąskaitos pavadinimas, kuriam buvo sukurta TDS operacija.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-125">**Name** – The name of the vendor, customer, or ledger account that the TDS transaction was created for.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-126">**Suma** – Sąskaitos suma, kuriai buvo suskaičiuota TDS.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-126">**Amount** – The invoice amount that the TDS was calculated on.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-127">**Mokesčio suma** – TDS mokesčio suma, apskaičiuota operacijai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-127">**Tax amount** – The TDS tax amount that was calculated for the transaction.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-128">**Sertifikato data** – TDS sertifikato data, kuri buvo atnaujinta TDS operacijai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-128">**Certificate date** – The TDS certificate date that was updated for the TDS transaction.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-129">**Sertifikato numeris** – TDS sertifikato numeris, kuris buvo atnaujinta TDS operacijai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-129">**Certificate number** – TDS certificate number that was updated for the TDS transaction.</span></span>

10. <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-130">Puslapyje **Atkurtini sertifikatai** pažymėkite **Uždaryta** žymimą laukelį, kad uždarytumėte TDS sertifikato numerį po to, kai užbaigėte TDS operacijų atnaujinimą **Sertifikato naujinimo** puslapyje.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-130">On the **Recoverable certificates** page, select the **Closed** check box to close the TDS certificate number after you've finished updating it for TDS transactions on the **Update certificate** page.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-131">Norėdami pasirinkti **Uždaryta** žymimą laukelį visiems įrašams puslapyje, rinkitės **Žymėti viską**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-131">To select the **Closed** check box for all records on the page, select **Mark all**.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
    > <span data-ttu-id="3e0d5-132">TDS sertifikatų numeriai, kurių **pasirinktas** žymės langelis Uždaryta, nepasiekiami puslapyje **Atnaujinti sertifikatą**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3e0d5-132">TDS certificate numbers that the **Closed** check box is selected for aren't available on the **Update certificate** page.</span></span>
