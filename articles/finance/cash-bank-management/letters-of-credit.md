# <a name="letters-of-credit"></a><span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-103">Akredityvai</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-103">Letters of credit</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-104">Akredityvai yra banko dokumentai, kurie paprastai naudojami prekių pardavimo ir pirkimo tarptautiniu mastu atvejais.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-104">Letters of credit are bank documents that are commonly used for the purchase and sale of goods across international borders.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-105">Akredityvai naudojami tarptautinėms operacijoms, siekiant užtikrinti, kad bus atlikti mokėjimai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-105">Letters of credit are used for international transactions to ensure that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-106">Akredityvas yra banko išduota sutartis, pagal kurią bankas sutinka užtikrinti mokėjimą pirkėjo vardu, jei sutarties tarp pirkėjo ir pardavėjo sąlygos yra patenkinamos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-106">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to ensure payment on behalf of the buyer, if the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-107">Atkreipkite dėmesį, kad akredityvas taip pat vadinamas dokumentuotu kreditu (DC).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-107">Note that a letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-108">Juridinis subjektas yra importo akredityvo pirkėjas arba pretendentas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-108">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-109">Juridinis subjektas yra eksporto akredityvo pardavėjas arba naudos gavėjas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-109">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-110">Toliau nurodytos su akredityvu susijusios šalys.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-110">The following parties are involved with a letter of credit:</span></span> 

 - <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-111">Pretendentas (pirkėjas), kuris ketina mokėti už prekes</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-111">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span> 
 - <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-112">Gavėjas (pardavėjas), kuris gaus mokėjimą</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-112">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-113">Išduodantysis bankas, kuris išduoda akredityvą</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-113">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-114">Konsultuojantis bankas, kuris vykdo operaciją pretendento vardu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-114">The advising bank that carries out the transaction on behalf of the applicant</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-115">Akredityve aprašomos prekės, bet kokie reikalingi dokumentai, siuntimo data ir galiojimo pabaigos data, po kurios mokėjimas nebus atliktas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-115">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment will not be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-116">Išduodantis bankas už akredityvą ima maržą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-116">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-117">Akredityvas gali būti atšaukiamas arba neatšaukiamas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-117">A letter of credit can be revocable or irrevocable.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-118">Akredityvo pobūdis gali būti perkeltinas, neperkeltinas arba generuojamas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-118">The nature of a letter of credit can be transferable, non transferable, or revolving.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b9e9b-119">Paprastai akredityvas yra neatšaukiama ir patvirtinta sutartis, kad, pateikus visus tikslius siuntimo dokumentus, konkrečiam naudos gavėjui bus atliktas mokėjimas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b9e9b-119">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>
