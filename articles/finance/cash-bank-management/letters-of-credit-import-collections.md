<a name="letters-of-credit"></a><span data-ttu-id="a4307-107">Akredityvai</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-107">Letters of credit</span></span>
-----------------

<span data-ttu-id="a4307-108">Akredityvai naudojami tarptautinėms operacijoms, siekiant užtikrinti, kad bus atlikti mokėjimai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-108">Letters of credit are used for international transactions and help guarantee that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a4307-109">Akredityvas yra banko išduota sutartis, pagal kurią bankas sutinka užtikrinti mokėjimą pirkėjo vardu, jei sutarties tarp pirkėjo ir pardavėjo sąlygos yra patenkinamos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-109">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to guarantee payment on behalf of a buyer, provided that the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a4307-110">Akredityvas taip pat vadinamas dokumentuotu kreditu (DC).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-110">A letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a4307-111">Juridinis subjektas yra importo akredityvo pirkėjas arba pretendentas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-111">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a4307-112">Juridinis subjektas yra eksporto akredityvo pardavėjas arba naudos gavėjas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-112">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a4307-113">Su akredityvu susijusios šios šalys:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-113">The following parties are involved in a letter of credit:</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="a4307-115">Gavėjas (pardavėjas), kuris gaus mokėjimą</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-115">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="a4307-116">Išduodantysis bankas, kuris išduoda akredityvą</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-116">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="a4307-118">Akredityvas apima prekių aprašymą, bet kokius reikalingus dokumentus, siuntimo datą ir galiojimo pabaigos datą, po kurios mokėjimas nebus atliktas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-118">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment won't be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a4307-119">Išduodantis bankas už akredityvą ima maržą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-119">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="a4307-120">Akredityvas gali būti **atšaukiamas** arba **neatšaukiamas**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-120">A letter of credit can be **revocable** or **irrevocable**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a4307-121">Akredityvo pobūdis gali būti **perkeltinas**, **neperkeltinas** arba **generuojamas**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-121">The nature of a letter of credit can be **transferable**, **non-transferable**, or **revolving**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a4307-122">Paprastai akredityvas yra neatšaukiama ir patvirtinta sutartis, kad, pateikus visus tikslius siuntimo dokumentus, konkrečiam naudos gavėjui bus atliktas mokėjimas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-122">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>

## <a name="import-collections"></a><span data-ttu-id="a4307-123">Imporo rinkiniai</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-123">Import collections</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="a4307-124">Importo dokumentų inkasavimas yra sutartis tarp banko ir eksportuotojo (pardavėjo), kai bankas sutinka pristatyti siuntimo dokumentus tarptautiniam importuotojui (pirkėjui).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-124">An import collection is an agreement between the bank and the exporter (seller), in which the bank agrees to deliver the shipping documentation to the international importer (buyer).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a4307-125">Iš banko tikimasi, kad jis pristatys siuntimo dokumentus, kai tik gaus užmokestį grynaisiais pinigais už išsiųstas prekes arba kai gaus pasirašytą dokumentą, kuris įrodo, kad mokėjimas įvykdytas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-125">The bank is expected to deliver the shipping documentation upon receipt of payment for the shipped goods in cash, or upon receipt of a signed draft toward the payment.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="a4307-126">Importo dokumentų inkasavimas padeda užtikrinti, kad pardavėjui bus sumokėta, kai pirkėjas gaus importuotų prekių pristatymo dokumentus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a4307-126">An import collection helps guarantee that the seller is paid when the buyer collects the shipping documents to take delivery of the imported goods.</span></span>
