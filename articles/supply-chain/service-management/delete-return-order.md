[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

1.  <span data-ttu-id="ac7a2-105">Spustelėkite **Pardavimas ir rinkodara** \> **Periodinis** \> **Valymas** \> **Naikinti grąžinimo užsakymus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ac7a2-105">Click **Sales and marketing** \> **Periodic** \> **Clean up** \> **Delete return orders**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="ac7a2-107">Nurodykite kriterijus ir spustelėję **Gerai** grįžkite į formą **Naikinti grąžinimo užsakymą**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ac7a2-107">Specify the criteria, and then click **OK** to return to the **Delete return order** form.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ac7a2-108">Kokie sukurti juridinio subjekto grąžinimo užsakymai bus pateikti, lemia nurodyti kriterijai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ac7a2-108">Return orders that have been created in the legal entity are displayed, based on the specified criteria.</span></span>
