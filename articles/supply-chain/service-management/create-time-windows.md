[!INCLUDE [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="43af2-104">Galite naudoti laiko langus, kad optimizuotumėte aptarnavimo užsakymo eilučių planavimą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="43af2-104">You can use time windows to optimize the scheduling of service order lines.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="43af2-105">Spustelėkite **Aptarnavimo valdymas** \> **Sąranka** \> **Aptarnavimo sutartys** \> **Laiko langas**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="43af2-105">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service agreements** \> **Time window**.</span></span>
