[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="d44c1-104">Eikite į **Aptarnavimo valdymas** \> **Sąranka** \> **Aptarnavimo abonementai** \> **Abonementų grupės**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d44c1-104">Go to **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **Subscription groups**.</span></span>
