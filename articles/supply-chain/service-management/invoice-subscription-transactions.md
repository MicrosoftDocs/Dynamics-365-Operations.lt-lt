# <a name="invoice-subscription-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="dbcb1-103">Abonementinės operacijos, kurioms išrašyta SF</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dbcb1-103">Invoice subscription transactions</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]



1.  <span data-ttu-id="dbcb1-104">Spustelėkite **Aptarnavimo valdymas** \> **Bendra** \> **Aptarnavimo abonementai** \> **Visi aptarnavimo abonementai**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dbcb1-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="dbcb1-105">Pasirinkite paslaugos abonementą, tada spustelėkite **Redaguoti** arba **Abonementas**, kad sukurtumėte paslaugos abonementą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dbcb1-105">Select a service subscription and then click **Edit** or click **Subscription** to create a service subscription.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="dbcb1-106">Spustelėkite **Analizuoti** \> **Mokesčių operacijos**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dbcb1-106">Click **Analyze** \> **Fee transactions**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="dbcb1-107">Pasirinkite vieną ar keletą mokesčio operacijų, kurių SF norite išrašyti, ir spustelėkite **Funkcijos** \> **SF pasiūlymas**, kad atidarytumėte formą **SF pasiūlymai**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dbcb1-107">Select the fee transaction or fee transactions to invoice, and then click **Functions** \> **Invoice proposal** to open the **Invoice proposals** form.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="dbcb1-108">Spustelėkite **Kurti SF** ir formoje **Kurti SF** įveskite tinkamus parametrus</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dbcb1-108">Click **Create invoice** and enter the appropriate parameters in the **Create invoice** form</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="dbcb1-109">Spustelėkite **Gerai**, kad formoje **SF pasiūlymai** sukurtumėte SF pasiūlymą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dbcb1-109">Click **OK** to create the invoice proposal in the **Invoice proposals** form.</span></span>

7.  <span data-ttu-id="dbcb1-110">Patvirtinkite SF turinį, norėdami registruoti SF spustelėkite **Registruoti SF**, kad atidarytumėte formą **Registruoti SF**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dbcb1-110">Verify the contents of the invoice, click **Post invoice** to open the **Post invoice** form to post the invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="dbcb1-111">Be to, SF galima išspausdinti iš formos **Registruoti SF**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="dbcb1-111">You can also print the invoice from the **Post invoice** form.</span></span>
