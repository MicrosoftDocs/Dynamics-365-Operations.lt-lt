[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="fdf83-104">Spustelėkite **Aptarnavimo valdymas** \> **Sąranka** \> **Aptarnavimo užsakymai** \> **Aptarnavimo etapai**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fdf83-104">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service orders** \> **Service stages**.</span></span>
