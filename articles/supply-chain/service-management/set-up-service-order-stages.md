[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="69154-104">Eikite į **Aptarnavimo valdymas** \> **Sąranka** \> **Aptarnavimo užsakymai** \> **Aptarnavimo etapai**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="69154-104">Go to **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service orders** \> **Service stages**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="69154-107">Pasirinkite atitinkamus etapo parametrus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="69154-107">Select the appropriate parameters for the stage.</span></span>
