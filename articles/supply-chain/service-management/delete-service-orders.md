# <a name="delete-service-orders"></a><span data-ttu-id="778d1-103">Naikinti aptarnavimo užsakymus</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="778d1-103">Delete service orders</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="778d1-104">Spustelėkite **Aptarnavimo valdymas** \> **Periodinis** \> **Aptarnavimo užsakymai** \> **Naikinti aptarnavimo užsakymus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="778d1-104">Click **Service management** \> **Periodic** \> **Service orders** \> **Delete service orders**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="778d1-105">Formoje **Naikinti aptarnavimo užsakymus** spustelėkite **Pasirinkti**, kad nurodytumėte kriterijus, pagal kuriuos bus panaikinami aptarnavimo užsakymai, tada spustelėkite **Gerai**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="778d1-105">On the **Delete service orders** form, click **Select** to specify the criteria to select the service orders to be deleted, and then click **OK**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="778d1-106">Pažymėkite žymės langelį **Rodyti sistemos pranešimą** norėdami sugeneruoti sistemos pranešimą, kuriame rodomi panaikinti aptarnavimo užsakymai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="778d1-106">Select the **Show Infolog** check box to generate an Infolog that displays the deleted service orders.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="778d1-107">Spustelėkite **GERAI**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="778d1-107">Click **OK**.</span></span>


> [!NOTE]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="778d1-108">Jei nenurodėte jokių kriterijų, pagal kuriuos parenkami aptarnavimo užsakymai, visi aptarnavimo užsakymai panaikinami.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="778d1-108">If you do not specify any criteria to select the service orders, all service orders are deleted.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="778d1-109">Tačiau, kai išeisite iš formos <STRONG>Naikinti aptarnavimo užsakymus</STRONG>, turėsite galimybę panaikinti visus aptarnavimo užsakymus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="778d1-109">However, when you exit the <STRONG>Delete service orders</STRONG> form, you will have the option to delete all service orders.</span></span></P>
> <P><span data-ttu-id="778d1-110">Be to, galėsite panaikinti tik tuos aptarnavimo užsakymus, kuriuos leidžiama panaikinti, atsižvelgiant į etapą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="778d1-110">Also, you can only delete service orders with a stage that lets you delete them.</span></span></P>
