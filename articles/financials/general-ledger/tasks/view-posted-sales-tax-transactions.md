[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="64574-104">Atsižvelgiant į tai, ko reikia, užregistruotas PVM operacijas galite peržiūrėti iš įvairių puslapių.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-104">You can view posted sales tax transactions from various page depending on what you need to view.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="64574-105">Šioje užduotyje naudojama demonstracinė įmonė USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-105">This task uses the USMF demo company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="64574-106">Eikite į Mokestis > Užklausos ir ataskaitos > PVM užklausos > Registruotas PVM.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-106">Go to Tax > Inquiries and reports > Sales tax inquiries > Posted sales tax.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="64574-107">Spustelėkite Rodyti filtrus.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-107">Click Show filters.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="64574-108">Uždarykite puslapį.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-108">Close the page.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="64574-109">Pasirinkite Mokestis > Netiesioginiai mokesčiai > PVM > PVM sudengimo laikotarpiai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-109">Go to Tax > Indirect taxes > Sales tax > Sales tax settlement periods.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="64574-110">Išplėskite dalį Laikotarpio intervalai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-110">Expand the Period intervals section.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="64574-111">Pasirinkite jus dominantį intervalą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-111">Select the interval you are interested in.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="64574-112">Rodyti pasirinkto sudengimo laikotarpio intervalo registruoto PVM operacijas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-112">Display posted sales tax transactions for the selected settlement period interval.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="64574-113">Registruoto PVM operacijų sąrašą galite filtruoti toliau.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-113">You can further filter the list of posted sales tax transactions.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="64574-114">Uždarykite puslapį.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-114">Close the page.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="64574-115">Pasirinkite Mokestis > Netiesioginiai mokesčiai > PVM > PVM kodai.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-115">Go to Tax > Indirect taxes > Sales tax > Sales tax codes.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="64574-116">Veiksmų srityje spustelėkite PVM kodas.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-116">On the Action Pane, click Sales tax code.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="64574-117">Spustelėkite Registruotas PVM.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="64574-117">Click Posted sales tax.</span></span>
