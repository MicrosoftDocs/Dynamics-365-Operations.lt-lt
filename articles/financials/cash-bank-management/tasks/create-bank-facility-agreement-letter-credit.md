11. <span data-ttu-id="4d318-118">Sąraše raskite ir pasirinkite norimą įrašą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4d318-118">In the list, find and select the desired record.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="4d318-119">Sąraše spustelėkite saitą pasirinktoje eilutėje.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4d318-119">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="4d318-120">Lauke Limitas įveskite su banku sutartą priemonės sumą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4d318-120">In the Limit field, enter the facility amount that was negotiated with the bank.</span></span>

18. <span data-ttu-id="4d318-125">Spustelėkite Pratęsti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4d318-125">Click Extend.</span></span>
19. <span data-ttu-id="4d318-126">Uždarykite puslapį.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4d318-126">Close the page.</span></span>
