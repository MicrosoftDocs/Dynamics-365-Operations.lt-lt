1.  <span data-ttu-id="92d09-118">Pasižymėkite „Dynamics 365“ puslapio **Projekto valdymas ir apskaita** \> **Tabeliai** \> **Mano tabeliai (optimizuoti mobiliesiems įrenginiams)** URL.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="92d09-118">Note the Dynamics 365 URL for the **Project management and accounting** \> **Timesheets** \> **My timesheets(Optimized for mobile)** page.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="92d09-120">Pasirinkite **Projekto valdymas ir apskaita** \> **Tabeliai** \> **Mano tabeliai (optimizuoti mobiliesiems įrenginiams)**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="92d09-120">Select **Project management and Accounting** \> **Timesheets** \> **My timesheets(Optimized for mobile)**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="92d09-121">Pasirinkite **Naujas**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="92d09-121">Select **New**.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="92d09-122">Pasirinkite išteklių.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="92d09-122">Select a resource.</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="92d09-123">Rodomos šios savaitės kalendoriaus datos.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="92d09-123">The calendar dates for the current week are shown.</span></span>

7.  <span data-ttu-id="92d09-124">Spustelėkite **Gerai**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="92d09-124">Click **OK**.</span></span>

14. <span data-ttu-id="92d09-131">Įvedę tabelio eilutes, pasirinkite **Darbo eiga** \> veiksmą **Pateikti**, kad savo tabelį pateiktumėte į darbo eigos patvirtinimo procesą.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="92d09-131">After you have completed entering the timesheet lines, select the **Workflow** \> **Submit** action to submit your timesheet to the workflow approval process.</span></span>
